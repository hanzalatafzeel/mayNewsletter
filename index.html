Session on Personal Finance & Equity Investments by Salman Quraishi

Date: 13th May.

"50 Ka Funda", an innovative investment-based event, organized by Sundarbans house, in Paradox 2024, designed to introduce participants and our IITM-BS student community engaged in tech-heavy backgrounds to the world of finance and investment, ...

considering the boom in this sector and the significance of the impact that individuals have or will have in the future stages of our lives. Over a period of 30 days, teams strategically invest in stock markets, virtual enterprise like paper trade, or in retail & E-commerce to grow their initial investment. With a total of 400+ event registration count (approx). We organized a super engaging mid-event online webinar by Salman Quraishi Sir, Head of Sales from Zerodha on investment strategies as an ice-breaker on Personal Finance & Equity Investments. Participants discovered insider tips and strategies to kickstart or elevate their financial journey. The webinar focussed on, the speaker giving inclusive insights on Investing Made Easy for Beginners, Personal Finance Investment Way, Stock Investment Ways and Tips for the Stock Market. Practical tips and strategies for successful stock investing were shared to enhance participants' understanding and decision-making abilities. This webinar provided valuable knowledge but also inspired and empowered our participants to make informed financial decisions, to unleash their wealth fundamentals! (which was also the event tagline!).

Investment, financial freedom and early retirement by Raman Pushkar sir

Date: 30th May.

Under the same event “50 Ka Funda" organized in Paradox 2024, an innovative investment-based event, the event culminated with a keynote address from a seasoned expert of the field on the IIT Madras Campus, i.e., Raman Pushkar Sir, Vice President of Standard Chartered, coach, consultant, mentor, finance influencer, speaker and trainer....

who shared insights on the outcomes of the month-long investment activities and provided foundational knowledge on equities, personal finance, early retirement and fund management. This immersive experience aimed to equip participants with practical skills and a deeper understanding of wealth fundamentals (which was also our event tagline!). Participants got hands-on experience regarding various financial facets like the fundamentals of the stock market (BSE/NSE/Nifty 50/SENSEX/Mutual Funds/ETFs/Forex etc.), personal finance and more. Key Highlights : Successful real-world application of investment principles, with some teams achieving about 40% return on their initial investment, and beginners getting a hands on brief with a live trade by our expert sessions online and on-campus. Enhanced financial literacy and practical investment experience for all participants, we delivered what we promised.

Paradox Badminton League: Sundarbans Smashers Triumph to Bronze
We are thrilled to share the exhilarating journey of the Sundarbans Smashers in the Paradox Badminton League, where we proudly clinched the bronze medal. Each match was a testament to our grit, determination, and team spirit. ...

Match 1: Sundarbans Smashers vs Namdapha

  • Praveen dominated the court, securing a resounding 11-1 victory as our trump card.
  • Aliza showcased her skills and emerged as the winner.
  • The dynamic duo of Hari and Krishay clinched a convincing win.
  • Ritik, despite a valiant effort, faced a tough opponent and lost.
  • The formidable pairing of Praveen and Aliza sealed the match with a victory.

Match 2: Sundarbans Smashers vs Unknown Rivals
  • Praveen once again proved his prowess with an 11-6 win as our trump.
  • Aliza faced a challenging match and, unfortunately, lost.
  • Hari and Krishay fought hard but were unable to secure a win.
  • Ritik bounced back with a stellar performance to win his match.
  • Aliza and Praveen teamed up to deliver a crucial win, keeping our hopes alive.

Match 3: Sundarbans Smashers vs Kaziranga (Semifinal)
  • Praveen led the charge with a 15-8 triumph, solidifying his role as our trump.
  • Aliza encountered a tough opponent and lost.
  • Praveen and Hari displayed exceptional coordination and clinched the win.
  • Ritik faced a formidable challenge and lost his match.
  • The pairing of Aliza and Krishay, despite their best efforts, fell short.

Final Bronze Match
  • Praveen, our star trump, delivered a crucial win.
  • Aliza fought valiantly but was unable to secure a win.
  • The partnership of Hari and Krishay brought us a vital victory.
  • Ritik, despite his relentless efforts, faced a tough loss.
  • Finally, Aliza and Praveen teamed up once more, sealing the match and our position on the podium with a win.

Through each match, the Sundarbans Smashers displayed unwavering determination and teamwork. Our journey to the bronze medal in the Paradox Badminton League was filled with memorable moments, thrilling victories, and lessons learned. We are immensely proud of our team's performance and look forward to continuing our pursuit of excellence in future tournaments.

Sundarbans at Qutopia

We're thrilled to share the exciting journey of our Sundarbans Team at the recent Qutopia event during Paradox. Despite our smaller team size, our enthusiasm and dedication shone brightly throughout each round. ...

Sundarbans at Qutopia:

  • Participants: Kushagra Srivastava and Mithilesh Ravi Shankaran reresented Sundarbans House with enthusiasm and skill.
  • Round 1: This round featured a solo quiz, where Kushagra and Mithilesh performed admirably. Despite being only two participants from our house, they were selected by default for the next round..
  • Round 2: Moving into the semi-finals, our team competed fiercely among 13 teams, ultimately securing a tie for 2nd place with Kanha House, each earning 70 points.
  • Final round: n the finals, facing off against 7 other teams, Sundarbans House showcased their knowledge across four challenging rounds. We finished with a commendable 85 points, won Bronze medal overall.

Congratulations to Kushagra and Mithilesh for their outstanding performance and representing Sundarbans House with such dedication.
